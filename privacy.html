THE HOLLOW GATE — PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Short version

The Hollow Gate does not collect any data from you. Not analytics, not crash reports, not advertising IDs, not your name, not your email, not your save games, not your location, not anything.

Longer version

What we collect

Nothing.

What we share

Nothing — because we collect nothing.

Local storage

The app saves your game progress to your own device using the operating system's standard local storage. This data never leaves your machine. It is not sent to us, to Apple beyond what the operating system does, or to any third party.

Payments

If you purchased The Hollow Gate from the Mac App Store, Apple handles the transaction under Apple's Privacy Policy — we do not see or store your payment information.

If you purchased the direct-download version through a Stripe payment link on our website, Stripe handles the transaction under Stripe's Privacy Policy. We do not see your card number. Stripe shares with us only your email address (so we can contact you about your purchase) and the fact of payment.

Children

The Hollow Gate is rated 12+. It is not directed at children under 12 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone of any age.

Third-party services

The Hollow Gate does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or trackers of any kind.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will live at this URL with an updated date at the top.
